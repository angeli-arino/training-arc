# Copy Style

Training Arc copy should sound warm, cute, and practical.

## Voice

- supportive
- lightly funny
- not cringe
- not medicalized
- not corporate
- not aggressively motivational

## Approved phrases

- Protect the protagonist.
- Tiny win accepted.
- Rest is training.
- Still showing up, just smarter.
- Main character energy detected.
- Your nervous system gets a vote.
- Consistency over perfection.
- Today’s arc still counts.

## Status copy

### Slay Day

Train as planned. Main character energy detected.

### Modify the Quest

Still showing up, just smarter. Reduce intensity today.

### Recovery Episode

Rest is training. Protect the protagonist.

### Peace Mode

Regulate first. Training can wait until your nervous system logs back in.

## Avoid

- hustle culture language
- shamey language
- “no excuses” language
- corporate productivity copy
- overly gamer-ish quest walls
