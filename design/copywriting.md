# Copywriting

## Voice

Training Arc speaks like a warm, funny, practical training buddy. It should be encouraging without being cringe, cute without being childish, and useful without sounding clinical.

## Core phrases

Use these consistently:

- Today’s Arc
- Body Scan Quest
- Cooked Meter
- Training Compass
- Slay Day
- Modify the Quest
- Recovery Episode
- Peace Mode
- Tiny win accepted
- Protect the protagonist

## Status names and meanings

### Slay Day

Meaning: Train as planned.

Tone:

- confident
- upbeat
- “you’re ready” energy

Example copy:

- “Main character energy detected.”
- “Train as planned.”
- “You’re clear to send it, sensibly.”

### Modify the Quest

Meaning: Reduce intensity, volume, or complexity.

Tone:

- practical
- validating
- no guilt

Example copy:

- “Still showing up, just smarter.”
- “Reduce the intensity and keep the habit alive.”
- “Tiny win accepted.”

### Recovery Episode

Meaning: Rest or active recovery.

Tone:

- protective
- warm
- firm but kind

Example copy:

- “Rest is training.”
- “Protect the protagonist.”
- “Today is for recovery, not proving anything.”

### Peace Mode

Meaning: Emotional regulation first.

Tone:

- grounding
- calm
- gentle

Example copy:

- “Regulate first. Training can wait.”
- “Your nervous system gets the main quest today.”
- “Start with one soft reset.”

## Home copy

Hero title options:

- “How cooked are we today?”
- “Ready to check the training vibes?”
- “Body scan before we send it?”

Hero subtitle options:

- “Quick check-in before you train, modify, or rest.”
- “Let’s protect the protagonist.”
- “One tiny scan, one smarter decision.”

CTA:

- “Start Body Scan Quest”

## Body Scan labels

Energy:

1. floor
2. low battery
3. functional
4. ready
5. main character

RPE:

1. chill
5. steady
10. boss fight

Soreness options:

- None
- Mild
- Medium
- Spicy
- Absolutely cooked

Breathing / illness options:

- Normal
- Slightly off
- Coughing
- Sick-sick

Mood options:

- Calm
- Frazzled
- Anxious
- Emotionally cooked

Planned workout options:

- Run
- Gym
- Hybrid
- Rest day
- Unsure

## Empty states

No scan yet:

- “No scan yet today. Let’s check in before the quest.”

No workouts logged:

- “No logs yet. Tiny wins count when you save them.”

No weekly arc data:

- “This week is still loading its character development.”

## Error states

Keep errors simple and kind:

- “Something glitched. Try again in a sec.”
- “Couldn’t save that arc. Your effort still counts.”
