# Product Vision

## Product name

Training Arc

## User

Training Arc is a personal, non-work app for Abby. It supports hybrid training, running, gym, soreness, fatigue, recovery, mood, race prep, and daily training decisions.

## Core question

“How cooked am I today, and should I train, modify, or rest?”

## Core experience

Training Arc should feel like a warm daily check-in that gives a practical decision quickly. It should reduce overthinking and help Abby protect consistency without guilt.

## Emotional promise

Training Arc should make Abby feel:

- guided, not judged
- cute but capable
- supported when tired
- encouraged when showing up
- protected from overtraining
- proud of tiny wins

## App personality

- cute fitness app
- cozy anime planner
- soft RPG / training arc companion
- Type A but chaotic
- warm, soft, practical, slightly playful
- polished mobile app feel

## What the app is not

Training Arc is not:

- a corporate dashboard
- an admin panel
- a gamified menu screen
- a hardcore athlete analytics platform
- a cold fitness tracker clone
- a productivity app with fitness stickers
- a plum/purple fantasy RPG UI

## MVP purpose

The MVP should focus on the daily training decision loop:

1. Open app.
2. See Today’s Arc.
3. Tap “Start Body Scan Quest”.
4. Answer quick scan questions.
5. Receive a clear recommendation.
6. Save the day.
7. See the week slowly build.

## MVP screens

1. Home Dashboard
2. Body Scan Quest
3. Training Decision Result
4. Workout Log
5. Weekly Arc View

## Success criteria

The app succeeds when Abby can open it when tired, sore, anxious, or unsure and quickly get a kind but useful answer.
